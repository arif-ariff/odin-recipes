Cottage Cheese Burrito

A cottage cheese burrito is a surprisingly tasty dish made with uncommon ingredients. Don't let the name fool you - it's nothing like a common burrito. It also packs a surprising amount of protein and is easy to make.
Ingredients:

  • Burrito-sized tortilla (corn or flour)
  • 2 oz cheddar cheese
  • 60g 2% cottage cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • Garlic powder
  • Chipotle seasoning
  • Salt
